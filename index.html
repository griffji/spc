Our Values

For being all about Jesus, we want the person and work of Jesus to be famous in our city, town and state. This fame begins in our own lives, and extends into our homes, workplaces, universities, city, town, and state.

Our Vision

To be the church that displays the love of Christ and connects with people of all walks of life through our creative services, discipleship, outreach, and the establishment of multiple campuses by streaming our Weekend Experience services globally.
